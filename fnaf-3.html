Five Nights at Freddy's 3 is the third installment in the Five Nights at Freddy's franchise, developed by Scott Cawthon. The game was released in 2015, and once again garnered attention from fans of the series due to its unique gameplay mechanics and lore.



In FNAF 3, the player takes on the role of a security guard who has been hired to work the night shift at a haunted attraction called Fazbear's Fright: The Horror Attraction. The attraction is built around the remains of the original Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, and the player must navigate through the dark, eerie corridors to survive the night.



One of the biggest changes in FNAF 3 is the removal of the animatronics as the primary threat. Instead, the player must deal with a single animatronic, Springtrap, who is possessed by the spirit of the infamous killer and former owner of the restaurant, William Afton.



To defend against Springtrap, the player has access to a variety of tools, including a camera system, audio devices, and vents. The player must use these tools strategically to monitor Springtrap's movements and avoid being caught by him.



The game also introduces new mechanics, such as the addition of a ventilation system that must be repaired periodically to prevent the player from suffocating. There are also hidden secrets and Easter eggs scattered throughout the game that help to uncover the deeper lore and backstory of the franchise.



One of the most significant additions to FNAF 3 is the inclusion of minigames. These mini-games are interspersed throughout the game and provide clues to the game's story and characters. The minigames are presented in a retro arcade style and offer a welcome break from the tense and terrifying gameplay of the main story.



The graphics and sound design in FNAF 3 are once again top-notch, with detailed and realistic animations, and an immersive soundscape that further adds to the game's atmosphere of dread and unease.



Overall, Five Nights at Freddy's 3 is another excellent addition to the franchise, offering a new and exciting gameplay experience while expanding upon the lore and story of the series. Fans of the series will find plenty to love in this game, and newcomers to the franchise will appreciate the engaging gameplay and spooky atmosphere.
