Getting Over It with Bennett Foddy is a popular game that has garnered a cult following since its release in 2017. The game is a platformer game with a unique twist - the player controls a man in a pot who has to climb a mountain using a sledgehammer. The game is known for its steep learning curve and high levels of difficulty, which has led to many players becoming addicted to it.



One of the key features of Getting Over It is its challenging gameplay. The game is designed to be difficult, with players required to navigate treacherous terrain, jump over obstacles, and avoid falling. However, what sets Getting Over It apart from other platformers is its use of physics. The game's mechanics are designed to mimic real-life physics, which makes it incredibly challenging and satisfying to play.
